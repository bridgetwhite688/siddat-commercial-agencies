Why choose Siddat

Siddat Commercial Agencies is the premier property management and real estate firm serving Makueni and Machakos counties. As a registered Kenyan company with license number 2T586901, we specialize in managing, buying, and selling land and plots with an unwavering commitment to excellence. Our slogan, “Trust, Value, and Service,” underscores our dedication to delivering unparalleled service and building lasting relationships with our clients.


We strive to create a strong and lasting relationship with our clients by providing excellent property management services, ensuring that all landlords have a positive experience. Our team of experienced professionals and a dedicated team of property agents work tirelessly to meet your needs and deliver exceptional property management.


At Siddat Commercial Agencies, we pride ourselves on maintaining the highest standards for the properties we manage. We ensure that all rental properties are kept in pristine condition and handle all rent collection duties on behalf of landlords.


Choose Siddat Commercial Agencies for reliable, professional service and exceptional property management.
